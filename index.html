Places to Visit

Opera House

I visited in December 3rd 2017

The Sydney Opera House is a multi-venue performing arts centre in Sydney. Located on the foreshore of Sydney Harbour, it is widely regarded as one of the world's most famous and distinctive buildings and a masterpiece of 20th century architecture. When I first visited the opera house I was with my cousins and we were just standing there looking at it for around 10 minutes until my dad finally got the tickets for us to go in. I don't recommend going inside because in my opinion its very boring in there. But outside at night its very cool to see the lights. They put amazing and mesmerising colored lights.



Sydney Harbour Bridge

I drove through in December 4th 2017

The Sydney Harbour Bridge is a steel through arch bridge in Sydney, spanning Sydney Harbour from the central business district (CBD) to the North Shore. The view of the bridge, the harbour, and the nearby Sydney Opera House is widely regarded as an iconic image of Sydney, and of Australia itself. Nicknamed "The Coathanger" because of its arch-based design, the bridge carries rail, vehicular, bicycle and pedestrian traffic.
